Box Breathing

A technique used by Navy SEALs for stress management

  • Inhale for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 4 seconds
  • Exhale for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 4 seconds

4-7-8 Breathing

A natural tranquilizer for the nervous system

  • Inhale for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 7 seconds
  • Exhale for 8 seconds

Deep Breathing

Simple yet powerful relaxation technique

  • Inhale deeply for 5 seconds
  • Hold briefly for 2 seconds
  • Exhale slowly for 5 seconds

Alternate Nostril

Balance your energy and calm your mind

  • Close right nostril, inhale left for 4s
  • Hold for 2s
  • Close left, exhale right for 4s
  • Inhale right for 4s
  • Hold for 2s
  • Exhale left for 4s
