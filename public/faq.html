Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Neuro Nurture

What is Neuro Nurture?

Neuro Nurture is a comprehensive mental health support platform that provides various tools, activities, and resources to help individuals manage their mental well-being. Our platform includes meditation exercises, art therapy, support groups, and emergency assistance.

Is Neuro Nurture free to use?

Basic features of Neuro Nurture are free for all users. Premium features are available through a subscription plan, which provides access to additional resources and personalized support.

How do I create an account?

Click the "Login" button in the top navigation and select "Sign Up". Fill in your details, verify your email, and you're ready to start using Neuro Nurture.

How do I reset my password?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. We'll send you instructions to reset your password securely.

What types of activities are available?

We offer various activities including guided meditation, breathing exercises, art therapy, journaling, and wellness games. Each activity is designed to support different aspects of mental health.

Can I track my progress?

Yes, you can track your progress through our wellness dashboard. It shows your activity completion, mood trends, and achievement milestones.

How do I join a support group?

Navigate to the Support Groups section, browse available groups, and click "Join Group". You can participate in discussions and connect with others sharing similar experiences.

What should I do in an emergency?

If you're experiencing a mental health emergency, click the "Emergency Help" button for immediate access to crisis resources and hotlines. In life-threatening situations, always call emergency services (911).

Is my information private?

Yes, we take privacy seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data without your explicit consent.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account and all associated data through your account settings. Once deleted, all your personal information will be permanently removed from our systems.
